## Objective

(1) Clarify that **pages are owned by the relevant platform/section** and document or implement that ownership. (2) **Fill Studio-related content** from v1 where still relevant, so Studio information is complete in v2.

## Scope

- **Ownership:** Define which section or "platform" owns which pages (e.g. Gateways, Orchestrators, Developers). Produce a table or document; optionally reflect in CODEOWNERS or a simple ownership map.
- **Studio fill:** Use the v1→v2 coverage report and any Studio inventory to identify missing or thin Studio content in v2. Add or complete Studio pages/sections from v1 material that is still relevant.
- Styling and structure must follow the style guide (CSS Custom Properties, existing components).

## Deliverables

- Ownership table or document (e.g. in docs or CONTRIBUTING) listing section/platform and the pages they own.
- Updated or new Studio content in v2, drawn from v1 where appropriate.
- Report at `docs/PLAN/reports/28-platform-ownership-and-studio-fill-report.md` summarising ownership decisions and what Studio content was added or changed.
