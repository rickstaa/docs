# Task RFP-M14: Handover ownership

**Missing idea:** M14  
**RFP deliverable:** (iv) Public Workflow — Notion wording

---

## Objective

Complete the **handover** of ownership and review process (not only “define”): transfer ownership to designated owners and document it so the RFP requirement “Define and **handover** ownership and review process” is met.

## Scope

- Notion (iv): “Define and **handover** ownership and review process for maintaining quality”
- Report: “Define ownership” — handover (transfer to owners) is not tracked
- Deliverable: ownership map + handover steps (who owns which sections, CODEOWNERS or equivalent, and confirmation that owners have been assigned and notified)

## Deliverables

- Ownership map: which person/role owns which doc section or area (e.g. Developers, Gateways, Orchestrators)
- Handover: CODEOWNERS file and/or Notion update assigning owners; optional short “handover completed” note (date, list of owners)
- Link from contribution guide or “How to propose changes” to the ownership map

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Ownership is defined per section/area
- [ ] Handover is done: owners are assigned and aware (e.g. CODEOWNERS + notification or doc update)
- [ ] RFP gap M14 is marked complete in the gaps report
